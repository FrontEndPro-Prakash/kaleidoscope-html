SEMINARS AND CONFERENCES

Overview

Our experienced team, led by Dr. David Guralnick,
runs courses, seminars, and conferences, both within organizations and in public venues.

Dr. Guralnick is president of the International
E-Learning Association, an adjunct professor at Columbia University, and a regular keynote speaker at events around the world.

Our seminars are geared to help individuals at a variety of levels—whether you are just getting started with online or mobile learning, or looking to improve your current offerings or explore different approaches.
