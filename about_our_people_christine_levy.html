Christine Levy
Vice President of Design
and Development

Christine Levy holds an M.A. from Northwestern University in Learning Sciences, a field that combines education and technology, and a B.S., also from Northwestern, in media design and production. Ms. Levy has designed and developed a variety of online educational and corporate training programs, focusing significantly on simulations and other forms of immersive, online scenario-based learning. Before joining Kaleidoscope, Christy was a Cognitive Engineer at Diamond Technology Partners, and a Content Director at Cognitive Arts Corporation. Ms. Levy began working at Kaleidoscope in 2000.
