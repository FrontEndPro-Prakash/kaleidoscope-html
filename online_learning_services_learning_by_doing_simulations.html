ONLINE LEARNING SERVICES

Learn-by-Doing Simulations

Research has shown that there's no better way to learn than by doing—the learning takes place in context, and skill transfer to the learner's job and workflow is smooth. Well-designed learn-by-doing simulations allow learners to gain and practice skills in a "safe" environment—an online world in which they can freely make mistakes without impacting the real-life business.

Our learn-by-doing simulation courses put learners in a realistic role and provide them with guidance to help make challenging decisions and feedback when things don't go as planned to help them understand what went wrong and how to handle similar situations in the future. The challenge aspect of a learning-by-doing environment provides the feeling of a game environment while maintaining a focus on learning.
